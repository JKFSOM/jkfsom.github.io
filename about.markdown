Outside of university I've utilised machine learning for a variety of tasks, including to extract field boundaries from satellite imagery, and generate realistic handwriting using an LSTM paired with a custom robotics platform. In the near future I would like to explore natural language generation (NLG) techniques.

I am always looking for exciting research and professional opportunities, so feel free to get in touch.
